Tiramisu

A coffee-flavored Italian dessert made with ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with mascarpone cheese and cocoa.

Cannoli

Sicilian pastry consisting of tube-shaped shells filled with sweet ricotta cheese and often decorated with pistachios or chocolate chips.

Panna Cotta

A creamy Italian dessert made with sweetened cream thickened with gelatin and molded, often served with berry coulis.

Gelato

Italian-style ice cream that is denser and more flavorful than regular ice cream, served at a slightly warmer temperature.
