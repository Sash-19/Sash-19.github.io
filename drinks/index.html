Aperol Spritz

A refreshing Italian cocktail made with Aperol, Prosecco, and soda water, typically garnished with an orange slice.

Limoncello

A traditional Italian lemon liqueur from Southern Italy, served chilled as a digestif after meals.

Negroni

A classic Italian cocktail consisting of equal parts gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth, garnished with an orange peel.

Chinotto

A bitter Italian soft drink made from the chinotto fruit, similar to cola but with a unique citrusy and herbal flavor.
